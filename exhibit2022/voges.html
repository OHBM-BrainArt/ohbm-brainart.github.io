Virgillo Voges

a patient in a psychiatric clinic, diagnosed with paranoid schizophrenia.

Introduction

Virgillo Voges's painting "desert room" depicts the situation of a person suffering from paranoid schizophrenia. The artwork was inspired by very personal sentiments of patients in a psychiatric hospital. Aim is to sensitize the observer to the specific disorder and to related circumstances.

Relation to "The Connected Brain"

Schizophrenia is a brain-related disorder. Various neurotransmitter systems and genetic influences are discussed as causing factors. People diagnosed with schizophrenia often feel a pathological disconnection from themselves and to others.

Fun Story

One of the key symbols in “desert room” is the plant in the background. It was inspired by a patient's nightmare, where beautiful flowers changed their qualities, transforming themselves into carnivorous plants soaring into the sky.
